GRAND DIWALI PERFORMANCE
NOVEMBER 6th 2026

MUSIC. DANCE. FOOD

This day, we’ll host our grand Diwali event. We love to host traditional performers from across Rajasthan.
Watch the dancers set up, apply traditional make up and dress in their finest most colourful silks.

Listen as the musicians practice and wonder at their instruments - some extraordinary, some simple,
each one a beautiful piece of craftmanship.

The haveli is a natural stage and forms a fine backdrop for performance, rich in sound and colour.
These performances are not just for our guests - who will soak up the culture of our state - but also for our local
people who come from far and wide to enjoy what the haveli has to offer.
